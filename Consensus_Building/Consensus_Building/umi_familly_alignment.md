# UMI family alignment 

**Tool:** MAFFT version 7.453

Parameters used:

### *--localpair*

Uses a sensitive local pairwise alignment strategy, improving alignment accuracy.



### **--maxiterate 500**

Performs iterative refinement for up to 500 cycles to optimize the multiple sequence alignment and improve overall alignment quality.


### **--reorder** 

Reorders sequences in the final alignment based on similarity, improving alignment organization and visualization.
